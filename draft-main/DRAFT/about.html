IT Skills Olympics is an annual event of the University of Makati's College of Computing and Information Sciences.

What is IT Skills Olympics?

This event celebrates the best and brightest young minds in the field of information technology. This prestigious competition is open to students from all over the country, and features a variety of challenging tasks in areas such as programming, cybersecurity, web page design, and more. This year's competition is expected to be even more competitive than ever before, with new challenges and a greater focus on real-world problem solving. The IT Olympics is a great way to celebrate the achievements of excellent IT students and to inspire the next generation of innovators.

The College of Computing and Information Sciences (CCIS) is the leading college in ICT education programs of the university by providing competitive, relevant and functional IT Curriculum responsive to the needs of the industrial and business organizations.


Furthermore, the College of Computing and Information Sciences has also been instrumental with the inclusion of the University of Makati in the 2023 World University Rankings for Innovation (WURI) through the shortlist Innovative Program (Rank 50 - 100).
